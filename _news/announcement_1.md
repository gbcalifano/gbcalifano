Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship awarded under Horizon Europe. The project, **GASTRO-X** (*Generative Artificial Intelligence for Sensory Trust and Resource Optimisation via Crossmodal Nudging*), will run for 24 months at the **University of Oxford**, Department of Experimental Psychology, supervised by [Prof. Charles Spence](https://www.psy.ox.ac.uk/people/charles-spence).

Fellowship start date: 16 September 2026. Proposal score: 96.8/100 (top ~9.6% success rate).
