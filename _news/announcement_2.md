New paper published in the *Stata Journal*: **"Relating latent class membership to covariates and outcomes: Two bias-adjusted methods in Stata"** (with Rosa Fabbricatore), *Stata Journal* 26(2), 153–176.

The paper introduces the `step3` command, implementing the BCH and ML corrections for classification error in three-step latent class analysis. The software is available on SSC: `ssc install step3`.

DOI: [10.1177/1536867X261449931](https://doi.org/10.1177/1536867X261449931)
